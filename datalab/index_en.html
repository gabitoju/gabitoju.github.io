Data Lab

Data Lab is an experimental space in analysis and data visualization. The idea is to use different techniques and libraries such as D3 to display data in a simple and intuitive way, always seeking to use the best technique.

This is a work in progress, so as time goes on I'm going to be adding new visualizations and analysis.

This are the available visualization and analysis:

  • The Lost Years New!
    How many years have been lost in traffic accidents in Uruguay between 2012 and 2013?
  • Appetite for Stats: Insights on the world's most dangerous band
    Visualizations, insights and stats on Guns N' Roses songs from the Live Era period (1987-1993).
